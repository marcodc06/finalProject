General Information

Welcome to Cossio Landscaping where we make beautiful outdoor spaces. Whether you want a peaceful garden, a cool backyard hangout, nicer looking yard, or just regular maintenance, we've got you covered. Our team knows how to make landscapes look amazing. Check out our work and let's talk about your dream outdoor space!

Cossio Landscaping is a family business here in Arizona. We brings a personal touch to every outdoor project. Founded by father-son team Jose and Marco Cossio, we're all about crafting beautiful landscapes that fit your home.

Jose has years of experience and he can create designs that blend seamlessly with nature. Jose has the wisdom of years in landscaping, while Marco is his assistant and keeps projects on track.
